Credits 
Featuring vertically scrolling centered
Arial Font credits of size 35; 
a hand drawn sprite animation of an
astronaut walking in space 
which consists of 6 poses
and a rocket landing sprite animation 
which consists of 13 poses 
created in Paint with a layer scrolling 
backgrounds containing hand drawn elements 
and graphical primitives created in Paint.

3D Animation made using Processing based
on the topic "Keele in Space"

FEATURES USED
- Hand drawn sprite animation
- Modular Arithmetic
- Layer Scrolling
- Multimedia

AUDIO USED
- Space Ambient Music 
- Relaxing music 
- Rocket Flying sound
- Rocket Landing sound
- Portal sound
